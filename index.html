Welcome to Mount Everest

Mount Everest, known in Nepali as "Sagarmatha," is Earth's highest mountain, standing at 8,848 meters above sea level. It attracts thousands of climbers from all over the world.

Discover more about this majestic peak, its history, challenges, and the culture surrounding the world's highest point.

Quick Facts

  • Height: 8,848 meters (29,029 feet)
  • First Ascent: May 29, 1953
  • Location: Nepal/Tibet Border
